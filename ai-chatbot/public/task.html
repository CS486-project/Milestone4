Your Task

Scenario

You and three friends are planning a trip to Europe together, but you each have very different priorities. One of you cares most about nightlife, another about history and culture, another has a tight budget, and the last has dietary restrictions (e.g., vegetarian or halal).

You will use an AI travel assistant to help your group reach a decision everyone can live with.

Your Goal

Using the shared travel documents and the AI system, agree on a single European city that works for everyone. Be ready to explain how the destination addresses each person's priority.

How to Approach the Task

You can use the AI system however you like — there is no single right way to get to a decision. Here are some ideas to get you started:

  • Compare destinations. Ask the AI to compare two or three of the cities on a specific dimension. For example: "How does Rome compare to Barcelona for travelers on a tight budget?"
  • Address each priority. Ask about each friend's must-have: "Which city has the best nightlife?", "Which has the most history?", "Which is most affordable?", "Which has the best vegetarian or halal options?"
  • Weigh trade-offs. Ask the AI to lay out the strengths and weaknesses of each city for the group as a whole. For example: "What does our group give up if we pick Amsterdam vs.\ Barcelona?"
  • Reach a decision. When you feel confident, choose one city and be ready to explain how it addresses each of the four friends' priorities.

You can upload documents, ask questions, and re-prompt as many times as you need. There is no right answer — we are interested in your reasoning process.
